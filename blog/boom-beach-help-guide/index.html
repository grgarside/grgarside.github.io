Boom Beach Help & Guide

This is the help and user guide for Boom Beach, an iOS (iPhone, iPad and iPod touch) and Android strategy game.
Boom Beach is a combat strategy game with a focus on multiplayer strategy with millions of players to attack and defend against as you explore a huge archipelago.
Available on the App Store and Google Play for free and rated ★★★★ (4.2) based on 3281 ratings.
