About TDA Enrichment

Always have access to Thomas Deacon Academy Enrichment with the official TDA Enrichment app.

  • Know where you need to be and who is running each enrichment activity, as well as which week/time the activity is running.
  • Keep up-to-date with changes to the timetable.
  • Get involved with different activities.
  • Understand resistance training and aerobic training, and the differences between them.
  • Learn about the correct way to warm up and cool down.
  • Read about nutrition and the frequently asked questions list.
