About TDA Planner

TDA Planner provides mobile access to the Thomas Deacon Academy website, allowing students to access information from the school's website on their mobile device, such as term dates and college information.

News can be added by the school for students to access on their iPhone, and push notifications can be used to notify students of important information.

Single sign on, from TDA Connect, is also used here to log in to the VLE from TDA Planner, providing students with their timetable and attendance on their mobile device. Login credentials can be transferred over iCloud between iPad and iPhone.

Features

  • Provides mobile access to the Thomas Deacon Academy website

    • Allows students to access information from the school's website on their mobile device

    • Information such as term dates are available for students

  • News can be added by the school for access to all students immediately on their mobile devices

  • Push notifications can be used notify students of important information

  • Information can be extracted from school servers, such as timetable and attendance

  • Single sign on for TDA Connect can be used here too
