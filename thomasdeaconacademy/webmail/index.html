About TDA Webmail

TDA Webmail is a service provided by the Thomas Deacon Academy which allows remote access to your emails from a web browser. This means you don't have to be on-site to access your emails, and can access them both on-site and off-site from a personal device including a mobile device.

Outlook Web App provides the web interface for this service. There are other ways to access email on your device. They are as follows:

  • TDA RDS allows you to open Outlook over RDS on your computer.
  • TDA Connect is an iPad app to open the dashboard, providing access to Outlook.
  • For iOS (iPhone or iPad), you can connect to the server through Mail.app. If you need help configuring this on your iPhone or iPad, speak to or email George Garside.

Students are limited to 200 MB of storage space for emails. You are encouraged to delete emails once you no longer require them. Spam will result in removal of the service.

Access to the Thomas Deacon Academy is subject to the Thomas Deacon Academy's ICT Code of Conduct.
