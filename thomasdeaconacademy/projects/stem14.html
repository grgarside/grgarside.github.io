What does STEM stand for?

STEM stands for Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths.

How does it feel being a STEM scholar?

I think it's extremely fun because you get to do loads of experiments and go on loads of trips.

It's a lot better because we can visualise things more easily.

I feel very privileged because we have so many opportunities that could enhance our learning.

What sort of things have you done?

We've done lots of experiments and we've been on lots of trips so we can see stuff instead of using a textbook.

We've gone on loads of trips for instance Flag Fen and the Space Centre and these sort of trips we take what we've learnt and then apply them to our learning in school.

We've been doing lots of experiments and visual learning and we've been on lots of trips as well to enhance our learning.

How has this helped you with your learning?

Because we've been identified as visual thinkers, we know that it's easier when we're doing experiments because we can actually see what's happening rather than just learning from a book.

It's helped me be able to visualise it more so I can understand it better and it's just a lot easier.

It helps a lot because we actually get to experience first-hand what it's like.

We went to the space centre trip to see how satellites work because we have a topic on satellites.

We went to Flag Fen and we learnt about stratigraphy and for ourselves we learnt to do an archeological dig and then we learnt about that and applied that to our learning here.
